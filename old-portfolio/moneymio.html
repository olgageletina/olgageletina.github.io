MoneyMio

Web Design

Winter - Spring 2016

MoneyMio is a personal finance resource featuring tools designed to empower customers. Visitors to MoneyMio’s site are encouraged to learn, to save, and to prosper. It is the only bilingual website dedicated to empowering Latinos with personal finance education.
In working with MoneyMio, I composed layouts for the Check Tool and Debt Payoff Calculator as well as consulted on a number of brand elements. My designs included mobile and desktop variations with sub-question, input field, and error scenarios. moneymio.com is slated to launch in September 2016
