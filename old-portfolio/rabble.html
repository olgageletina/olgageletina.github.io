rabble

Web Development, Photography

Spring 2016

Rabble is an impact-investing platform that connects people with projects that improve society. Through rabble, anyone can invest in projects that align with their values. From revitalizing low-income neighborhoods, closing the gender gap or reducing carbon emissions, all rabble projects aim to deliver financial, social or environmental returns.
As a freelance web designer and developer, I created rabble's first website using a combination of a Wordpress theme, HTML, and CSS. The deliverables included a splash, about, and FAQ pages. The site was launched on April 14, 2016. In addition to the website, I conducted a team photo shoot and provided key aesthetic guidance.
