Fruit, and such

Video

Fall 2015

Healthy eating habits are a very important topic. Particularly, in the US where obesity and eating disorders are heavily discoursed. While these conditions are serious, my opinion is such that the related discourse doesn't have to be. On the contrary, I find that effectiveness is a result of a ludic experience, not a pedantic one.
Humor is big aspect of who I am. It makes my days memorable and my interactions enjoyable. Yet, humor wasn't a big part of my work. Until now…
Directed and filmed by Efraim Klein, written by me, starring Natalya Nyn and Stan Rozenraukh Fruit, and Such is a series of absurd skits - showcasing organic matter in an awkward fashion.
