Photography

Triptychs

Spring 2014 - Present

My photographs typically consist of several [color] variations on a single object. In this way I hope to harmoniously unify yet underscore the visual differences. The format of a triptych helps to facilitate this mission. By viewing the congruous yet disparate pieces side by side the viewer is challenged to reconcile and accept the differences between the three works.
